Approach (CRISP-DM)


Business Understanding – Identified the core question: which food products perform well vs poorly, and why?
Data Understanding – Explored the dataset to understand product categories, performance metrics, and surrounding context.
Data Preparation – Cleaned and structured the data for reliable analysis in Excel.
Modeling/Analysis – Pinpointed which data points were weak or unreliable depending on the surrounding location/environment, and how that affected product performance.
Evaluation – Validated findings against the original business question.
Deployment – Built clear visualizations in Excel to communicate the results.


Key Insight

Identified why certain food products succeed or underperform, tracing the issue back to specific data points that behave differently depending on surrounding location conditions — and corrected for that to get a more accurate picture.

Why This Project

While the dataset and instructions were provided, the problem framing, analysis path, and conclusions were entirely my own — turning raw FMCG data into a clear story of what works, what doesn't, and why.
